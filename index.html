About Dr. Niranjan Hiranandani

Dr. Niranjan Hiranandani is a pioneering force in India’s real estate sector and the Co-Founder of the Hiranandani Group. His journey in real estate began with a bold, long-term vision: to create fully integrated townships that combine residential, commercial, retail, educational, and healthcare infrastructure within a single, master-planned ecosystem. This vision materialized with the development of Hiranandani Gardens in Mumbai, a landmark project that transformed a quarry site into one of India’s most recognized urban communities and set new benchmarks for construction quality, planning precision, and asset appreciation.

Building on this foundation, Dr. Hiranandani expanded the township model across strategic growth corridors including Thane, Panvel, and Chennai, reinforcing his reputation for disciplined execution and infrastructure-led development. Over four decades, his real estate journey has been defined by consistency, credibility, and large-scale urban transformation, positioning him as one of India’s most respected and enduring real estate leaders.
